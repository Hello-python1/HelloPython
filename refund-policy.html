    Refund Eligibility

    Our refund policy is designed to provide you with the flexibility and peace of mind when enrolling in our courses. You may request a refund within 14 days of purchase if:

    • You have not accessed any course material.
    • You have not completed more than 20% of the course.

    How to Request a Refund

    To request a refund, please contact our support team at support@HelloPython.com. Include your order details and the reason for the refund.

    Processing Time

    Refund requests will be processed within 7-10 business days after approval.

    We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
